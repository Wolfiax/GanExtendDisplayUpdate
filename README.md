Character Hover Display
•	Line 1 – Level, danger indicator, rarity symbols, NPC name, and strength comparison to the player
•	Line 2 - HP, SP, MP, DV, PV, and Speed
•	Line 3 - Food, Weight, EXP, Work, and Hobby information
•	Line 4 – 8 Core attributes (STR, CON, DEX, PER, LRN, WIL, MAG, and CHR)
•	Line 5 - Resistances
•	Line 6 – Affinity to the player and NPC’s favorite gift (Check Images)
•	Line 7 - Conditions, buffs, and debuffs
•	Line 8 - Acts 
•	Line 9 - Passive feats
Ground Items and Equipment
•	Displays item level and rarity.
•	Shows material information.
•	Displays enchantment values numerically.
•	Works with equipment and gene-style items.
Controls
Input	Effect
Hold Alt	Reveal hidden lines
Alt + Caps Lock	Toggle always-show hidden lines
Alt + End	Toggle the mod on/off
Alt + Home	Toggle the game debug display
